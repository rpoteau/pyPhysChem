💡 Solution: The model that learns poorly

1. Find the logical error in this line of code. What correction should be made?

The error lies in the argument test_size=0.80.
Correction: The proportion must be reversed to test_size=0.20 (which will automatically allocate 80% of the data to training and 20% to testing).

2. Explain why this error causes very poor performance for the algorithm (What happens during learning?)

By setting 0.80, the student reserved 80% of the data for evaluation and only 20% for training. The algorithm simply did not see enough examples to understand the "chemistry" of the wine: this is what is called underfitting. Having been unable to derive reliable mathematical rules during its training, it is incapable of generalizing and fails to generalize to the remaining 80%.
